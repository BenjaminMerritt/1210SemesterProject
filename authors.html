About the Site:

Mission Statement

This website is designed to allow volunteers of the ETSU Eagle cams to easily schedule and participate in camera operations while allowing the university to manage and maintain volunteer access.

About

This website was created for the ETSU Eagle Cameras Project in the Spring of 2021 by two computing students in the web development class. The page was designed with ETSU's standards in mind as well as the needs of our volunteers. For questions or concerns about the website, please contact the creators at merrittb@etsu.edu.
